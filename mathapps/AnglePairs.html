Vocabulary
  • Adjacent angles: share a vertex and one side
  • Vertical angles: opposite angles formed by two intersecting lines
  • Linear pair: adjacent angles that form a straight line
  • Complementary: sum is 90°
  • Supplementary: sum is 180°
