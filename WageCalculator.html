How it works
- Weekly to yearly uses 52 weeks per year.
- Bi-weekly uses 26 pay periods per year.
- Monthly values are based on yearly ÷ 12.
- Net pay = gross pay × (1 - total deduction rate).
- If you enter net pay, gross pay is calculated backward from the total deduction rate.
- Hourly equivalent = weekly pay ÷ hours per week entered for equivalents.
- Deduction categories are shown separately in the yearly breakdown table.
